    Setup Notes

    • No-auth local SMTP: Host localhost, Port 25, leave Username/Password blank.
    • MailHog (dev testing): Host localhost, Port 1025.
    • Gmail / Outlook: These require TLS/STARTTLS. Use a local relay such as stunnel, msmtp, or sendmail that handles TLS and forwards plain connections to this app.
    • Office SMTP relay: Use your internal relay host and port — usually no auth needed.
